Liverpool vs Southampton - Carabao Cup Quarter-Final 2024

Match Summary

Date: December 18, 2024

Competition: Carabao Cup Quarter-Final

Venue: St Mary's Stadium, Southampton

Final Score: Southampton 1-2 Liverpool

First Half

Liverpool took an early lead with goals from Darwin Núñez and Harvey Elliott. Núñez capitalized on a defensive error to open the scoring, followed by Elliott's strike to double the advantage.

Second Half

Southampton mounted a strong comeback, with Cameron Archer pulling one back for the hosts. Despite sustained pressure and a late penalty appeal that was denied, Liverpool held firm to secure the win.
